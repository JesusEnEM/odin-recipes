  1. Put yeast into warm water with the sugar to activate for 15 minutes
  2. Mix yeast water and flour in a large mixing bowl unitl the dough is not sticky
  3. Cover the bowl and let the dough rest for 30 minutes, the dough will expand in size
  4. In the meantime, combine your spices to make an Italian Blend, or buy it premade
  5. Put more flour on kneading surface so the dough does not stick
  6. Knead out dough into a large circle and crimpe the edges to creat a crust
  7. Spread marinara sauce onto the circular dough, along with some of the seasoning
  8. Place Mozarilla cheese ontop the sauce unitl most of the surface is covered
  9. Put rest of the seasoning onto the cheese
  10. Place into oven for about 20 minutes at 400 F
  11. Take it out carefull let it cooldown for about 15 minutes
  12. Then cut into 8 equal slices and enjoy
