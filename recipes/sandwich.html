  • Three slices of bread
  • Mayo
  • Ham
  • Lettuce
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  1. Grab two of the slices of bread and put mayo on one side of each slice
  2. Put ham and sprinkle some salt and pepper on the ham
  3. Put lettuce on the ham and place the mayo side of the other bread slice on the lettuce
  4. Mayo the topside of the sandwich and one side of the third slice of bread
  5. Put another ham slice down ontop of the sandwich and sprinkle more salt and pepper
  6. Put more lettuce ontop of the ham and place last slice ontop mayo side down
  7. Cut in half and enjoy
